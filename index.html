Inspiration

Our first inspiration was taken from Everdell, as we wanted some kind of worker placement and resource management style of game. This quickly changed, but we kept looking to Everdell for story and narrative elements throughout our rule book. Carcassonne and Subterra were our inspirations for tile placement as we took “fog of war” elements from these types of map building games. Root and Clank were also games we looked to for various elements.
